# Benchmark Test Case 1: B2B Meeting Intelligence Assistant

## Raw Startup Idea
"Build an AI assistant that joins sales calls, summarizes next steps, and updates CRM automatically for SMB sales teams."

## Expected Artifacts
1. **Problem definition** focused on CRM hygiene gaps and post-call admin burden.
2. **Target user** centered on SMB account executives and sales managers.
3. **MoSCoW priorities** with strict MVP (capture call summary + action items + CRM push).
4. **7-day roadmap** that includes ingestion, summarization, CRM integration, QA, and launch.
5. **KPI set** including summary accuracy, CRM completion rate, and weekly active reps.
6. **Risk analysis** including privacy, hallucinations, and integration reliability.
